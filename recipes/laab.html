Lao Laab

Laab is one of the national dishes of Laos.

Basically, it's meat salad. In a good way. It could be chicken, pork, or beef, as long as it's diced up, mixed with greens, herbs, fish sauce, and garnished. Originating in Laos, it is eaten by the Lao people as a part of a traditional meal with other foods such as sticky rice, vegetables, or fermented fish sauces.

Today, we will show you how to make Laab in your own home. This recipe will be for chicken Laab. It is one of the easier varients to make with readily available ingredients.
